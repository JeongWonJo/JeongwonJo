Jeongwon Jo, PhD candidate

Hello! I am a PhD candidate at College of Information Sciences and Technology at Pennsylvania State University under the supervision of Dr. John M. Carroll.
Before that, I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Technology Management at Underwood International College at Yonsei University.
‍
I am a UX researcher with a distinctive knack for incorporating qualitative methods and Machine Learning and Data Science principles into my insights, leveraging human-centered design and data-driven strategies.

Explore my portfolio to see how I blend the best of these disciplines to understand users and propose practical design solutions.
